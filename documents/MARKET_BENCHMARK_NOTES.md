## Consistent Genre Patterns
- Triple-match sorting on shelves/cabinets with 3D goods theme.
- Simple single-finger interaction and fast feedback loops.
- Booster-heavy progression (shuffle/refresh/freeze/time tools).
- Level-based structure with increasing complexity.
- Casual puzzle positioning with relaxation plus strategy.

## User Feedback Signals Found
- High frustration around ad pressure and interruption frequency.
- Players want reliable progress persistence after reinstall/device change.
- Technical stability and control responsiveness are critical.
- Positive sentiment for frequent content updates and events.

## Alignment Check: Shelf Rush
- Implemented: core triple-match shelf loop.
- Implemented: undo + shuffle + purchasable extra shuffle booster.
- Implemented: persistent profile with missions/achievements.
- Implemented: settings toggles and polished home/game UX.
- Implemented: CI quality gates and release checklist.
